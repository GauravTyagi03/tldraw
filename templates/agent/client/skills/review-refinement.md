- Review the actual canvas state before declaring multi-step work complete.
- Look specifically for overlapping text, arrows intersecting text, arrow-to-arrow crossings, disconnected arrows, uneven spacing, and accidental misalignment.
- Prefer targeted fixes over redrawing the whole canvas.
- If the linter flags issues, decide whether they matter visually and fix the ones that do.
- End with a concise message describing the practical result, not every internal step.
